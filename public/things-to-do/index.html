Featured Fort Worth Attractions

Fort Worth Stockyards

Historic cattle drives, rodeos, and Western culture. Experience authentic Texas history in this iconic entertainment district.

Learn More
Where the Old West comes alive

Billy Bob's Texas

The world's largest honky-tonk featuring live country music, dancing, and authentic Texas nightlife entertainment. Bull riding every Friday and Saturday night!

Learn More
World's largest honky-tonk - Photo by Scott McKittrick

Globe Life Field

Home of the Texas Rangers, this state-of-the-art baseball stadium offers world-class sporting events and entertainment.

Learn More
Home of the Texas Rangers

Modern Art Museum of Fort Worth

Contemporary art in a stunning glass building designed by Tadao Ando, featuring rotating exhibitions and a permanent collection. Free on Fridays, half-price on Sunday. Right next to the Kimbell and Amon Carter museums.

Learn More
Contemporary art in architectural beauty

Trinity Trails

Miles of hiking and biking paths along the Trinity River, perfect for outdoor enthusiasts looking to explore Fort Worth's natural beauty. Be sure to check out the Clearfork Farmers Market on Saturdays.

Learn More
Scenic trails perfect for hiking and biking

Historic & Cultural

  • Kimbell Art Museum - World-renowned art collection in architectural masterpiece
  • Amon Carter Museum - American art specializing in Western and contemporary works
  • National Cowgirl Museum - Celebrating women of the American West
  • Fort Worth Museum of Science and History - Interactive exhibits and planetarium
  • Fort Worth Zoo - One of the top zoos in the nation

Entertainment & Events

  • Dickies Arena - Major concerts, sports, and events
  • Bass Performance Hall - Broadway shows and performing arts
  • Sundance Square - Downtown entertainment district with dining and shopping

Professional Sports

  • Dallas Cowboys - NFL games at AT&T Stadium in Arlington (30 minutes)
  • Texas Rangers - MLB games at Globe Life Field in Arlington (30 minutes)
  • Dallas Mavericks - NBA games at American Airlines Center in Dallas (45 minutes)
  • Dallas Stars - NHL games at American Airlines Center in Dallas (45 minutes)

Family Fun

  • Six Flags Over Texas - Thrilling roller coasters and rides in Arlington (30 minutes)
  • Hurricane Harbor - Water park adjacent to Six Flags (30 minutes)
  • Fort Worth Nature Center - Wildlife viewing and nature trails
  • Fort Worth Botanic Garden - Beautiful gardens and peaceful walking paths

Dining & Shopping

Local Favorites

  • Joe T. Garcia's - Famous Tex-Mex institution
  • Cattlemen's Steak House - Authentic steakhouse in the Stockyards
  • Spiral Diner - Plant-based comfort food

Owner's Favorite Coffee Shops

  • Avoca Coffee - Cozy coffee house off Magnolia Avenue
  • Cherry Coffee - Local favorite on Magnolia Avenue
  • Portico - Great coffee near the Stockyards
  • Cafe Azul - Charming spot near the Stockyards
